# 🇻🇳 Vietnamese Meme Collection: Độ Mixi 

## Who is Độ Mixi?
Độ Mixi (Phùng Thanh Độ) is one of the most famous streamers in Vietnam. Known for his humorous personality, iconic voice, and chaotic gaming streams, he has become a huge meme source in the Vietnamese community. His ethnicity is Tày (minor ethnic in Vietnam). His hometown is Cao Bằng.

---

## Classic Độ Mixi Memes

### 1. "Tày thế!"
**Meaning:** "Damn it good!"  
**Usage:** When something good in an unexpected way.

### 2. "Tù ngay!"
**Meaning:** "Jail now"  
**Usage:** When something funnily bad.

### 3. "Con chó cao bằng bộ PC"
**Meaning:** "The dog is as tall as the PC"  
**Why it funny:** "cao bằng (as tall as) is where Độ Mixi hometown is. Con chó cao bằng also mean dog of cao bằng, which is the same as calling Độ Mixi dog. In addition, bộ PC sound similar to Độ Mixi in vietnamese. In other word, the whole sentence, in vietnamese, can sound like: Do Mi Xi the Cao Bang dog"
**Usage:** When someone mention dog or PC.

### 4. "Toang rồi anh em ơi!"
**Meaning:** "It's over, guys!"  
**Usage:** When everything goes wrong  
**Common in:** PUBG, GTA, or any fail moment

### 5. "Đậu Miso"
**Meaning:** "Miso tofu soup"  
**Usage:** Occasionally replace Độ Mixi, mispoke Độ Mixi name.
**Why it funny:** Sound like Độ Mixi in vietnamese.
