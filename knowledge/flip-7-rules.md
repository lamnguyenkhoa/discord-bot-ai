# Flip 7 – Board Game Rules

## Overview
Flip 7 is a fast-paced card game focused on risk-taking, probability, and timing. Players draw cards and decide whether to continue or stop, aiming to collect points without busting.

- Players: 2–6
- Playtime: ~15–25 minutes
- Core mechanic: Push-your-luck

---

## Objective
The goal of Flip 7 is to be the first player to reach the target score (commonly 200 points, or agreed upon before the game).

Players accumulate points by drawing numbered cards and avoiding duplicates in a round.

---

## Components
- Deck of cards containing:
  - Number cards (1–12, with duplicates depending on value)
  - Special cards:
    - Freeze cards
    - Flip Three cards
    - Second Chance cards

---

## Setup
1. Shuffle the deck thoroughly.
2. Each player starts with 0 points.
3. Choose a starting player randomly.
4. Play proceeds clockwise.

---

## Gameplay Loop

### Turn Structure
On your turn:
1. Draw the top card from the deck.
2. Resolve the card effect immediately.
3. Decide:
   - **Continue** drawing (push your luck), or
   - **Stop** and bank your points.

---

## Card Types and Effects

### Number Cards
- Add the value to your round score.
- If you draw a duplicate number already in your current set → **you bust**.
- Busting means:
  - Lose all points for this round.
  - Turn ends immediately.

---

### Freeze Cards
- Force another player to take a card.
- That player must resolve it immediately.
- Can cause them to bust.

---

### Flip Three Cards
- Draw three additional cards in sequence.
- Resolve each card one by one.
- Risk increases significantly.

---

### Second Chance Cards
- Protect you from one bust.
- If you draw a duplicate:
  - You may discard the duplicate instead of busting.
  - The Second Chance card is then used up.

---

## Ending a Turn
A turn ends when:
- The player chooses to stop and bank points, or
- The player busts.

---

## Scoring

### Round Scoring
- Sum of all unique number cards collected during the turn.
- Only counted if the player does NOT bust.

### Bonus Rule (Flip 7)
- If a player collects **7 unique numbers** in a single round:
  - They achieve a “Flip 7”
  - Receive a bonus (commonly +15 points or agreed value)

---

## Winning the Game
- The game ends when a player reaches or exceeds the target score.
- The player with the highest score wins.

---

## Strategy Notes (for RAG enrichment)

### Risk Management
- Early stopping ensures consistent scoring.
- Aggressive play can yield high rewards but frequent busts.

### Card Awareness
- Tracking seen numbers reduces duplicate risk.
- High-value cards are rarer and riskier.

### Special Card Timing
- Use Freeze cards strategically to pressure opponents.
- Flip Three can swing the game but is high risk.
