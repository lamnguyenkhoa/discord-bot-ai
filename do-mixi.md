# Độ Mixi - Streamer & Meme Culture

## About Độ Mixi

**Real Name:** Phùng Thanh Độ  
**Born:** September 12, 1989  
**Birthplace:** Cao Bằng, Vietnam  
**Ethnicity:** Tày  

Độ Mixi is one of the "Tứ hoàng" (Four Kings) of Vietnamese streamers. He started streaming in 2016-2017 after working as an office worker. He gained fame through PUBG streaming and founded Refund Gaming team.

**Platform Stats:**
- YouTube: ~8.2 million subscribers
- TikTok: ~7.2 million followers

**Nicknames:** Tộc trưởng (Tribe Leader), Lươn Cao Bằng, Neymar Cao Bằng
