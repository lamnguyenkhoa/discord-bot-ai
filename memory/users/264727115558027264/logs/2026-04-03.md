User (Catatouille): Give full rules for flip 7
Bot: here’s the full Flip 7 rules, cleaned up:

**goal**
- be the first player to reach **200 points**.

**setup**
- shuffle the deck.
- each player gets **1 card face up** to start their round.
- keep the remaining deck in the middle.

**card types**
- **number cards**: 0–12, with duplicates in the deck for lower numbers and only one of each high number.
- **special cards**:
  - **Flip 2 / Flip 3**: make you draw 2 or 3 more cards, one at a time
  - **Freeze**: ends your turn immediately
  - **Second Chance**: lets you ignore one bust
  - **Modifier cards** like **+2, +4, x2** affect scoring

**how your turn works**
on your turn, you choose one:
1. **Hit** — draw the top card and flip it face up
2. **Stay** — stop and bank your round score

if you draw:
- a **duplicate number card** of one you already have, you **bust**
- if you have a **Second Chance**, you can use it to ignore that bust
- special cards resolve immediately

**busting**
- if you bust, you score **0 points for that round**
- your turn ends right away

**staying**
- if you stay, you keep all valid cards you’ve flipped that round
- those points are added to your score

**round end**
a round ends when:
- every player has either **stayed** or **busted**, or
- someone successfully gets **7 different number cards** without busting

that’s the famous **Flip 7**

**scoring**
- add up the value of your number cards
- then apply modifiers if you have them
- **x2** doubles your score before flat bonuses
- **+2 / +4 / etc.** are added after multipliers
- special/action cards usually don’t count as points unless the rules say otherwise

**winning**
- when someone reaches **200+ points**, finish the current round
- highest score wins
- if tied, keep playing rounds until someone wins outright
